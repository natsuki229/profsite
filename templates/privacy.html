・Responding to inquiries and customer questions
About the secure management of personal information
our website will take necessary and appropriate measures to prevent the leakage, loss, or damage of the handled personal information, as well as ensure its overall security management.
About entrusting personal information
When our website entrusts all or part of the handling of personal information to a third party, we conduct a rigorous investigation of the third party to ensure that the entrusted personal information is securely managed. We also provide necessary and appropriate supervision to the third party to ensure the security of the entrusted personal information.
